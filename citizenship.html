CITIZENSHIP

To become eligible to apply for Citizenship of Canada, you must have been physically present in Canada for total number of required days, in last 5 years, before the date of application.

Also, if you have been in Canada as a Temporary Resident, before obtaining Permanent Residency; you can also add allowed number of temporary residence days.

We, at KB Immigration Inc., access your eligibility to apply for Citizenship of Canada and ensure the completeness and accuracy of application preparation and timely submission, after having carefully accessed your eligibility requirements in aspect of age, language proficiency, taxes, employment and relevant presence.
